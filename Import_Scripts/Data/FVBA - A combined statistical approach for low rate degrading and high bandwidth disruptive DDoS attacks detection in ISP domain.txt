Flow-Volume based DDoS detection approach ( FVBA )

In this paper , a novel Flow-Volume based DDoS detection approach ( FVBA ) is proposed , which deals with detection of variety of DDoS attacks by monitoring the propagation of abrupt traffic changes inside ISP Domain .
Different attack scenarios are implemented by varying total number of zombie machines and at different attack strengths to measure effectiveness of proposed approach .
